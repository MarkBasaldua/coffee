Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Ethiopian Yirgacheffe beans are a hipster favorite for their bright, floral, and citrusy notes, making them perfect for summer brews. Sourced from the lush coffee-growing regions of Ethiopia, these beans offer a vibrant and complex flavor profile that pairs beautifully with cold brews and iced lattes. The natural processing method of Yirgacheffe beans enhances their fruity undertones, ensuring a light and invigorating coffee experience that stands out in the warm months.

Panamanian Geisha

The luxurious Panamanian Geisha beans. Celebrated for their delicate and nuanced flavors, including jasmine, bergamot, and tropical fruit notes, these beans are grown in the high altitudes of Panama. The meticulous harvesting and processing methods ensure the highest quality, making Panamanian Geisha a prized possession among coffee enthusiasts. A warm cup of this coffee offers a sophisticated and aromatic experience, perfect for cozy autumn mornings.

Colombian Supremo

The Colombian Supremo coffee bean is renowned for its balanced flavor and smooth, rich complexity. Sourced from the high elevations of the Colombian Andes, these beans are noted for their larger size and consistent quality. This coffee offers a medium-bodied experience with a crisp, fruity acidity that is accompanied by notes of caramel and nuts. The subtle undertones of herbal nuances make it an all-day coffee that is both refreshing and invigorating. Ideal for those who appreciate a coffee that delivers a full sensory pleasure without overpowering bitterness, making it perfect for a serene morning start or a gentle afternoon break.
